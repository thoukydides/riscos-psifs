PsiFS: Frequently Asked Questions

!SerialDev Not Seen

Why are InternalPC and SP_DualPC the only serial blockdrivers offered?

PsiFS is supplied with copies of the InternalPC and SP_DualPC serial block drivers. These two blockdrivers support most of the common configurations.

To use other blockdrivers, for example if an unusual serial port card is being used, then !SerialDev must be seen by the filer before PsiFS is loaded. The easiest way to do this on a machine running RISC OS 3.5 or later is to place it in the !Boot.Resources directory.

Please note that !SerialDev is not distributed with PsiFS; it must be obtained from another source.

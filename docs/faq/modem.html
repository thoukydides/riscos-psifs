PsiFS: Frequently Asked Questions

Online Media Internal Modem

Why is the error SWI &83901 not known displayed when using *PsiFSListDrivers?

The serial block driver for Online Media internal modem cards is badly written. Any attempt to initialise the block driver on a machine without the card fitted results in an error that bypasses the normal error handling mechanism. This prevents *PsiFSListDrivers from listing all of the available drivers.

To avoid this problem, open the !SerialDev.Modules directory, and remove the IntModem directory.

PsiFS: Frequently Asked Questions

Unsuitable Dearchiver

Why do links between the help pages not work?

The PsiFS distribution archive is produced using !SparkFS; problems may be experienced if other tools are used to dearchive the files. In particular, the "/" (forward slash) character used within the names of these help files could be converted to an "_" (underscore), resulting in the links failing to work.

To avoid potential problems, all of the files should be extracted using either !SparkFS or !SparkPlug by David Pilling.

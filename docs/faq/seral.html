Early Archimedes Serial Ports

Why does PsiFS not work on early Archimedes?

Early Acorn Archimedes computers had faulty serial ports that required cables wired differently to operate correctly. The Acorn A-Link should work reliably with these machines, but the Psion 3-Link and simple RS-232 serial cable require an adaptor wired as follows:
Acorn Archimedes connector
(9 pin female)		Psion 3-Link or RS-232 serial cable
(9 pin male)
2 (RxD)2 (RxD)
3 (TxD)3 (TxD)
1 (DCD) + 4 (DTR)4 (DTR)
5 (GND)5 (GND)
8 (CTS)6 (DSR)
7 (RTS)7 (RTS)
6 (DSR)8 (CTS)

To summarise, pins 6 and 8 should be swapped, pins 1 and 4 should be joined at the Acorn Archimedes end, and pins 1 and 9 are not used. The Internal block driver should then be used rather than InternalPC.

Dates and Times

A direct mapping is performed between RISC OS and SIBO or EPOC dates and times. However, SIBO and EPOC only store file date stamps as an exact multiple of two seconds. Hence, copying a RISC OS file may result in the file date stamp being rounded down to give an even number of seconds. Date stamps are always accurately converted in the opposite direction.
